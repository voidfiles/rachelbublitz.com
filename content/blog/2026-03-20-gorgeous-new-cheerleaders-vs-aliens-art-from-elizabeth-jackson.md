*Original art by Elizabeth Jackson.*

One of my best friends growing up lived two streets away from me. It was one of those situations where opposites make a great pair: one quiet reserved artist, one loud obnoxious drama nerd. back when I used to write (*awful*) poetry, we dreamed of her illustrations and my poems being published together.

We reconnected a few years, and found out that we're both still creating. I told her about my plays, my [book](https://www.yourstagepartners.com/how-to-build-a-playwright-in-three-acts) (*!*), and she told me she's still drawing and painting, and working to become an art teacher. Then, I got the best surprise in the mail, she sent me this incredible original piece inspired by [*Cheerleaders VS. Aliens*](https://www.pioneerdrama.com/SearchDetail.asp?pc=CHEERLEADE&id=0)! Isn't it gorgeous? When I was getting it framed it was *so* hard to pick a frame, because every color looked incredible. Maybe instead of poetry and illustrations we should team up and play around with a graphic novel?

Huge thank you to Elizabeth Jackson, I'm so glad we are back in one another's lives!

Happy Friday!
